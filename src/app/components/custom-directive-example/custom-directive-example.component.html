NOTE:: Directives are the functions which will execute whenever the Angular compiler finds it. Angular Directives enhance the capability of HTML elements by attaching custom behaviors to the DOM. Angular directives are categorized into three categories.
  • Attribute ex: ngClass, ngStyle
  • Structural ex: *ngFor, *ngFor
  • Component
